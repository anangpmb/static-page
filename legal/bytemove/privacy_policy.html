Privacy Policy

ByteMove — Logic Puzzle Game

Effective date: January 1, 2025
Last updated: June 2, 2026
Summary: ByteMove is an offline puzzle game designed to be safe for all ages, including children. We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Advertisements are served via Google AdMob and are configured in child-directed mode (COPPA / GDPR compliant): no behavioral or personalized ads, no Advertising ID tracking, and content is restricted to General Audience (G-rated) only. All game progress is stored locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

ByteMove does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. You can play the game completely anonymously.

1.2 Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored on your device only:

  • Game progress (current level per difficulty, completed levels)
  • Best stats per level (best moves, best time)
  • Player profile (XP, player level, achievements)
  • Daily challenge history and streaks
  • App preferences (language, sound, music settings)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it using the Export feature in the app settings.

1.3 Information Collected by Third Parties (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Because this app is designed for children, AdMob is configured in child-directed treatment mode with the following settings:

  • tagForChildDirectedTreatment = true — Google treats all ad requests as COPPA-compliant. Interest-based and remarketing ads are disabled.
  • tagForUnderAgeOfConsent = true — Additional GDPR protections apply for users under 13 in the European Economic Area.
  • maxAdContentRating = G — Ad content is restricted to General Audience only. No PG, Teen, or Mature ad content is served.

In child-directed mode, AdMob does not use the Advertising ID for behavioral or interest-based targeting. AdMob may still collect minimal non-personal technical data required to serve contextual ads (e.g. device type, OS version, IP-based approximate country). This data is processed by Google in accordance with their policies.

For details, see Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy and Google's Families Policy: https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9900633

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain game functionality
  • Save your progress and preferences locally
  • Show relevant advertisements (via AdMob)
  • Improve game performance and fix bugs

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. The only data sharing occurs through:

  • Google AdMob for ad serving (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Your explicit action when you use the Export/Share features to backup your progress or share achievements

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

ByteMove is designed to be a safe and educational game suitable for children of all ages. We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and, where applicable, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provisions for minors.

4.1 No Personal Information Collected from Children

We do not knowingly collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) from children under 13. The app does not require registration, does not ask for a name, email address, phone number, or any other personal information from any user.

4.2 Child-Safe Advertising

All advertisements displayed in the app are configured for child-directed treatment. This means:

  • No behavioral, interest-based, or remarketing ads are shown
  • The Advertising ID is not used for tracking or profiling
  • Ad content is restricted to General Audience (G-rated) only
  • Google's Families Ad Program guidelines apply to all ads served

4.3 Parental Rights (COPPA)

Parents and legal guardians have the following rights regarding their child's use of this app:

  • Review: Request to review any information collected by or associated with your child by contacting us at the email address below.
  • Delete: All game data stored locally can be deleted by the child/parent using the "Reset All Progress" option in Settings, or by uninstalling the app. No data is stored on our servers.
  • Refuse further collection: Since we do not collect personal information, there is nothing to opt out of on our end. To limit AdMob's data collection, you can reset or disable the Advertising ID in your device settings (see Section 5.1).
  • In-App Purchases: The app offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase. Parents should use device parental controls (Google Play Family Library, Apple Screen Time / Ask to Buy) to require approval before any purchase is completed.

4.4 Contact for Children's Privacy Concerns

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to this app, or if you have any concerns about our practices regarding children's privacy, please contact us immediately at: secretbaseprimary@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days and take appropriate action.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Opt out of personalized ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's Advertising ID settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising

5.2 Delete your data

You can delete all locally-stored game data by:

  • Using the "Reset All Progress" button in Settings → Statistics
  • Uninstalling the app (this removes all local data)
  • Clearing app data via your device's app management settings

5.3 Export your data

You can export your game progress at any time using the Export feature (Settings → Backup & Share). This generates a file containing all your progress that you can save or transfer to another device.

6. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using your device's standard secure storage mechanisms (e.g., SharedPreferences on Android, UserDefaults on iOS). We do not transmit your game data to any server.

7. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Internet: Required for AdMob to load advertisements
  • Camera (optional): Only used when scanning a QR code to import progress from another device. Not used otherwise.
  • Storage (optional): Only used when you export progress or save a screenshot

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Significant changes will be communicated through an in-app notification.
